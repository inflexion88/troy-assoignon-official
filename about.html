Why founders work with me

I’m Troy Assoignon — a positioning expert who started on construction sites, learned marketing during midnight shifts, and now helps founders command premium pricing, close complex deals, and raise capital. Positioning aligns offer design, sales, and investor narratives. When it’s vague, you get commoditized. When it’s precise, you defend margins and win better deals.

Every engagement I run delivers three things: a crystal-clear strategic position, documented proof other teams can deploy, and internal adoption so positioning doesn’t fade once the workshop ends.

My 20-day story (condensed)

  • Debt trenches & drywall dust

    I ran crews across Winnipeg, kept twenty families paid, and watched a $90K invoice disappear weeks before Christmas. That pain taught me weak positioning forces you to compete on price.

  • Night-shift education

    I wore one headphone on site, absorbed marketing frameworks at midnight, and scraped together over $10K to join a mentor when cash was tight. That leap moved me from drywall to strategy.

  • Community builder era

    We launched an entrepreneur collective that topped 10,000 members, secured Audi and Bacardi sponsors, and gave founders a space to be honest about positioning and pricing. Listening there shaped the diagnostics I still use.

  • Alecia & the Florida reset

    Alecia (now my wife) invited me on a road trip through Florida. We camped on beaches, slept in Walmart parking lots, and mapped the life we wanted. That commitment took us to Mexico, Southeast Asia, and dozens of boardrooms.

  • Early advisory transformations

    A service business jumped from -$300 to +$4K monthly cash flow. A construction client lifted contract values from $3K to $20K. A coach scaled from $800 retainers to $50K engagements. Documenting those wins became a habit.

  • Bali partnership scar

    A partner vanished mid-delivery. I refunded, restructured, and finished the program solo. It cemented my rule: no strategy leaves my shop unless I can defend it myself.

  • Nomad resilience

    We came home to a trashed Vancouver condo, rebuilt, then moved to Thailand with two suitcases. I ran investor trainings from Chiang Mai rooftops while untangling lost wires. Chaos forced me to build the systems clients rely on today.

  • Near-death clarity

    I flipped my truck three times after a 24-hour workday. Hanging upside down bleeding reminded me the operator is the asset. Now I hold myself — and every founder I coach — to a holistic standard: health, proof, and positioning move together.

How engagements work

Clients hire me to stop competing on price, secure investor confidence, or reset a chaotic go-to-market. Here’s what we do:

  1. Define the territory. Research, interviews, and leadership workshops to pinpoint the exact space you should own.
  2. Design the proof + narrative. Offers, pricing ladders, case studies, sales scripts, and investor decks that all reinforce the position.
  3. Defend it internally. Enablement kits, governance rituals, and adoption playbooks so positioning stays sharp months later.

When needed I bring trusted collaborators (RevOps, media buying, design), but I stay accountable for positioning integrity and proof.
