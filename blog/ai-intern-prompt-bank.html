Prompt Vault

AI Prompt Bank for SME Intern Pods

Use these prompts and QA questions to ensure AI-generated work reinforces your positioning narrative.

Prompt Sets

1. Positioning Refresh

  • “From this transcript, what category does the buyer place us in? Suggest 3 category reframes.”
  • “Convert these technical differentiators into business outcomes for a CFO persona.”
  • “Draft three proof hooks (stat, testimonial, use case) defending premium pricing.”

2. Content Pipeline

  • “Outline a 1,200-word article on ‘Fractional CMO vs Agency’ using Define/Design/Defend.”
  • “Generate a LinkedIn carousel with 5 slides teaching the diagnostic question #7.”
  • “Suggest email subject lines for the ‘AI Intern Pod’ offer, mixing urgency + specificity.”

3. Campaign QA

  • “Review this landing page as a skeptical COO. Highlight unclear positioning.”
  • “Rewrite this CTA block using diagnostic framing and scarcity.”
  • “Highlight jargon that would confuse non-technical founders and suggest alternatives.”

4. Executive Reporting

  • “Summarize this sprint board into 5 CEO-ready bullets including KPI deltas.”
  • “Explain why we paused Campaign X in two sentences for the board deck.”
  • “Create a quote that sells the diagnostic to investors.”

QA Rubric

  1. Does it restate our positioning pillar + ICP within the first 100 words?
  2. Is there at least one proof element (stat/testimonial/use case)?
  3. Does the CTA align with the diagnostic stage we target?
  4. Is tone decisive and expert? Remove filler words and hedging.
