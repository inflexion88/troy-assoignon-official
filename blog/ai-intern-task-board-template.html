AI Intern Task Board Template

Use this Kanban structure inside Notion/ClickUp. Each card carries the positioning pillar, ICP, and owner.

Board Layout

Column Purpose Required Fields
Positioning Backlog Ideas filtered through the positioning thesis. Pillar, ICP, KPI, link to positioning doc.
AI Draft Intern runs the prompt and attaches raw output. Prompt link, tool, due date, reviewer.
Human QA Fractional CMO reviews positioning, proof, compliance. QA checklist, edits required, approval toggle.
Scheduled / Live Publish, add KPI guardrails, schedule retro. Channel link, go-live date, metric owner, review date.

Weekly Checklist

  1. Groom backlog with the founding team; reject tasks that don’t support the positioning strategy.
  2. Assign each intern one AI draft + one analysis/report card per sprint.
  3. Use comments inside “Human QA” for feedback rather than Slack to keep context.
  4. During retro, archive completed cards and log KPI deltas in your cost model.
