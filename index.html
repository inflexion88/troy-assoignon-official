ABOUT THE EXPERT

Meet Troy Assoignon

Positioning Expert With Over 15+ Years Experience

FEATURED PROFILE
September 2025

Engineering Perception & Positioning

By Editorial Team

Troy Assoignon started on the jobsite, not in an agency. By 23 he was running crews, writing bids, and carrying $20K bi-weekly payrolls, learning estimating, pricing, and risk where mistakes cost real money. When he outgrew construction, marketing became the exit ramp, and he moved that discipline into positioning, similar logic applied to new markets.

Today Troy is a top positioning expert and strategist for C-Suite and founders who want revenue clarity, not hype. He has personally managed millions in ad spend for personal brands, creators, and industry leaders across multiple sectors.

Results include engineering a real estate fund narrative to raise $4.5M and leading a humanitarian initiative that generated $619,000 which funded evacuations for 2,750 women and children from a war torn territory. He designs a powerful positioning strategy for the situation at hand and works with teams to install it.

Troy's method is construction grade. Scope the work. Manage the project. Align the talent. Ship on time.

He advises select entrepreneurs and industry leaders, specializing in identifying unique market differentiators and developing strategic frameworks that convert this positioning into revenue-generating assets.
